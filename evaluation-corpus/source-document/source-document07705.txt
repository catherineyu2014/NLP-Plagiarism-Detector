  Continue
to make it manifest that all which is pure and lovely and of good repute in womanhood is entirely
compatible with the exercise of the rights of citizenship, and the performance of the duties
which we all owe to our homes and our country.  Confident that you will do this, and with no
doubt or misgiving as to your success, I bid you Godspeed.  I find I have written to the association
rather than to thyself, but as one of the principal originators and most faithful supporters,
it was very natural that I should identify thee with it.
